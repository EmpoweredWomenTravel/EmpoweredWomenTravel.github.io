Whether enjoying bustling city life, peaceful countrysides, sisterhood, salsa & sunsets or riding elephants, shopping on floating markets, relaxing massages or visiting golden temples… We have something for everyone.

Empowered Women…

Thank you for considering us to be one of your travel partners. Our trips are designed for anyone who wants a soul-stirring vacation. These are not women-only trips. They are designed to empower women at each of our destinations by supporting women-owned businesses. We have designed our tours to accommodate everyone from the solo traveler, to families and couples. It's designed for you to explore and connect with other people and get out of your comfort zone.

Empower Other Women

This trip gives you the opportunity to support other women. Studies show that when women are able to support themselves financially, children are better educated and families flourish. Our goal is to help women by supporting independently owned women businesses. We stay at their hotels and bed and breakfasts, visit their galleries, stores and much more. We have cultural exchange meals that allow us to intimately get to know the women at each of our destinations. We start our trips with a celebration. We celebrate and get to know each other. Our goal is to help you leave your trip lighter with more energy. We want you to step out of your comfort zone and do something that excites you in a good way.

You Are Much More Than a Reservation Number

Maximum trip size: 18 people.

This is your opportunity to meet people from all over the world who, like you, are willing and open to explore new territory — not as a tourist looking in. We are not spectators in the countries we visit. We are participators.

Though we are not backpacking our way through the countries we visit and sleeping in tents, this trip is an immersion into the cultural heritage of the places we travel. This trip also gives us the opportunity to empower mothers, sisters and daughters in the countries we visit, while at the same time being inspired by them.

You Are One of Our Travel Companions

We are with you every step of the way, putting your interest first, co-creating a lifetime experience.

This travel adventure includes:

  • Accommodations (Boutique Hotels and/or Bed and Breakfasts owned by women)
  • Cultural immersion at each destination
  • Breakfast daily

  • Bilingual, local tour guides in addition to our ground staff
  • Cultural experiences in support of local women at each destination
