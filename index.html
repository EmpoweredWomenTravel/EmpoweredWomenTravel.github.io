Empowered Women Travel

Not only do we travel — empowered women empower women. We travel to explore, inspire and renew our minds, bodies and spirits. We travel to expand our horizons and world views. Sometimes we do it alone, other times with friends. Often times we take these trips as a symbol of empowerment. Travel gifts us the chance to make connections with new people. These experiences are priceless; often lasting longer than material things and changing us in ways “things” cannot.

We are a travel and tour company created to give women and families interesting, inspiring travel encounters; however, we also do more. Our tours support local woman-owned businesses and are designed to give our travel partners more than a vacation. During our tours we spend time meeting local women. We also support local children, have a meal together and get to know people in the places we visit in a more intimate environment.

This trip is for you if:

  • You are interested in immersing yourself in other cultures.
  • You're ready to step out of your comfort zone.
  • You are ready to be inspired and an inspiration to others!
  • You are willing to be a part of a movement that supports women and strengthens communities.
  • You want to step up your Instagram and Facebook game with interesting pictures and stories.

Destination — Cuba

This trip takes you to the heart and soul of Cuba. Not for average tourists, this is a trip for explorers. It's an experience of a lifetime, one where you may meet a lifelong friend and career connections. One day, you'll look back and say “Yes!! I did that!” and have the pictures to prove it. We love Cuba because it's such a dichotomy. It's one of few Caribbean islands with a vibrant bustling city landscape and sleepy country facade, all within minutes of each other. It is home to more than 300 beaches spread across the Southern Caribbean Coastline.

Havana, its capital, is home to over 2 million people. The birthplace of the best cigars, rum and music in the world. It is a city that never sleeps! There is something for everyone in Cuba. The people are warm, the sand is soft and the music is muy caliente. This island is absolutely magical. Empowered Women Travel's mission is to explore this mystical country and support the women & children of Cuba, through humanitarian donations and financial support by shopping at their stores, and donating school supplies to their children.
