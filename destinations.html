Coming Soon — Where We're Headed Next

Every Empowered Women Travel destination is chosen the same way: incredible culture, unforgettable beauty, and women-owned businesses we can celebrate and support. Here's what's on our map — and you get a vote. Email us your pick and we'll build the next adventure around the most-requested destination.

The Africa Series

Africa Series

Rwanda

The land of a thousand hills — and the most literally "empowered women" country on Earth: Rwanda's parliament is majority female, the only one in the world. Kigali is one of Africa's safest, cleanest capitals. Women-run cooperatives, coffee farms, and the once-in-a-lifetime option to trek mountain gorillas.

Vote Rwanda
Africa Series

Morocco

Marrakech medinas, Sahara sunsets, mint tea and mosaic courtyards. We'll visit the famous women-run argan oil cooperatives of the Atlas foothills, shop women artisans in the souks, and sleep in riads that feel like a storybook.

Vote Morocco
Africa Series

Senegal

Dakar's music, markets and seaside energy — and the pilgrimage to Gorée Island, the UNESCO memorial to the transatlantic slave trade. Vibrant, French-flavored West Africa with a thriving women's textile and fashion scene.

Vote Senegal
Africa Series

South Africa

Cape Town is one of the most beautiful cities on the planet — Table Mountain, the Winelands, penguins at Boulders Beach. We'll tour women-owned wine estates and township enterprises, and stand where Nelson Mandela made history.

Vote South Africa
Africa Series

Kenya

The classic safari — sunrise over the Maasai Mara, elephants against the horizon — paired with visits to the women's beading cooperatives and village enterprises that are rewriting who benefits from tourism in East Africa.

Vote Kenya

Coming Soon

Thailand

Golden temples, floating markets, island sunsets and the best street food on Earth. Bangkok's markets are famously run by women — we'll shop them, cook with them, and finish with a beach day in the Andaman Sea.

Vote Thailand
Coming Soon

Bali, Indonesia

Rice terraces, water temples, flower baths and sound healing — the world's wellness capital. Bali's markets and family compounds run on women's enterprise, and its retreat culture was practically made for our sisterhood.

Vote Bali
Coming Soon

Cartagena, Colombia

A short flight from the U.S. to a walled city of flower-draped balconies, salsa nights and Afro-Caribbean heritage. We'll meet the palenqueras, dance until late, and day-trip to the Rosario Islands.

Vote Cartagena
Coming Soon

Brazil

Rio's beaches and Sugarloaf views, then Salvador da Bahia — the heart of Afro-Brazilian culture: capoeira, candomblé heritage, and the iconic Baianas whose food and traditions define the city. Rhythm, color and connection.

Vote Brazil
Coming Soon

Santorini, Greece

The bucket-list classic: whitewashed villages over a volcanic caldera, wine that grows in ash, sunsets in Oia that stop conversation. Add Athens for history and you have the perfect first trip to Europe.

Vote Santorini
